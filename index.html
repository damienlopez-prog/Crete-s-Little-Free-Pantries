About Us

Crete’s Little Free Pantries began in 2021 with a mission to provide food, hygiene items, books, and pet supplies to anyone in need. We operate with the philosophy that everyone deserves access to essentials with dignity and privacy.

Find a Pantry

  • 8th & Main – Blessing Box & Buddy Box (Pet Pantry)
  • 19th & Forest Ave – Food Pantry
  • 12th & Linden – Food Pantry
  • 9th & Main Ave – Little Library
  • 11th & Hawthorn – Little Library

All pantries and libraries are open 24/7.

How to Help

For Individuals

  • Donate food, hygiene items, or pet supplies at any pantry.
  • Share our posts on social media to spread the word.

For Organizations

  • Host a donation drive.
  • Promote our mission on your platforms.

Community Impact

Our pantries serve youth, families, and seniors in Crete. With 12.3% of residents living below the poverty line, and many relying on assistance, our mission is more important than ever. Buddy Box ensures pets stay with their families too.
