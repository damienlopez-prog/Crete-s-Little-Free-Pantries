About Us

Crete’s Little Free Pantries began in 2021 as a community project dedicated to offering food, hygiene items, and pet supplies to anyone in need. The Buddy Box, our pet pantry, honors a beloved dog and has become one of our most popular boxes.

Find a Pantry

All of our pantries and the Buddy Box are open 24/7 throughout Crete. You can find them near the following intersections.

  • Mini Food Pantry – Corner of 9th & Forest Ave
  • Blessing Box – Corner of 8th & Main Ave
  • Buddy Box (Pet Pantry) – Corner of 8th & Main Ave
  • Mini Food Pantry – Corner of 12th & Linden Ave

How to Help

For Individuals

  • Donate food, hygiene items, or pet supplies anytime—our boxes are open 24/7.
  • Share our posts on Facebook to help spread awareness.
  • Volunteer to restock boxes or help with community drives.

For Organizations

  • Host a donation drive to support the pantries or Buddy Box.
  • Share our mission on your social media or company website.
  • Contact us to discuss partnerships or sponsorships.
